Request Funding from VGSA

Please read the FAQ below before applying for funding. If you need assistance be sure to contact the Vice President of Finance

Examples of applications and decisions may be found at the bottom of this page.

VGSA Funding Guidelines
  • All applications must comply with the GSG Finance Laws and Funding Policy in addition to the VGSA specific guidelines below
  • The applying Student Organizations must be directly related to Viterbi School of Engineering OR indicate that the proposed event will have an attendance breakdown of at least 50% Viterbi students
  • The event MUST be open and free to attend for all Viterbi Students
  • Students organizing the event are NOT allowed to charge the participants in any way
  • The decision of the VGSA Board will be final and non-negotiable
  • You must apply 4 weeks prior to your event date
  • The VGSA Committee reviews funding requests once a week and will get back to you with the verdict at the end of the weekly cycle
  • T-shirts and hard durables (ie. grill, electronics, clothing, etc.) cannot be funded
If your event is approved for funding applicant or group will be required/expected to do the following
  • Appropriately affix the official VGSA logo to all promotional materials for the event
  • Promotional material must be visible within all Viterbi department (ie. email, flyers, facebook, etc)

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it typically take to receive funding after the date of submission of Funding Request?

The funding is only processed in the form of a P-card (University Credit Card) , Purchase Order (PO) , or Internal Requisition (IR) by GSG (Graduate Student Government).

It takes two weeks after submission of the Expense Request after approval from GSG to receive the funding in the above mentioned form.

It is imperative that the funding application is submitted 3 weeks prior to the event date otherwise it may lead to significant delay.

How do I know which vendors to use?

Contact the GSG Finance Intern (uscgsgfin@gmail.com). He/She will be able to provide you with which vendors can be used. Not all vendors are registered or USC approved.

Why am I being partially funded?

Due to budgetary constraints towards the end of the fiscal year VGSA funds get exhausted and your event may not receive the entire amount requested. This is a decision made by a panel of VSGA Board members based on the current resources available at the time and will be judicious in nature.
